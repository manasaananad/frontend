Got Questions?

We've heard them all.
Here's the truth.

Can I study abroad with a low CGPA (below 7.5)?
Absolutely yes — and this is one of the biggest myths we bust every day. Hundreds of our students with CGPAs between 6.0 and 7.5 have gotten into great universities in the US, UK, Canada, Germany, and Ireland. A low CGPA can be compensated by a strong GRE/IELTS score, a compelling SOP, solid work experience, or research projects. Our counselors know exactly which universities are the right fit for your specific profile.
How much does studying abroad actually cost? Is it worth it?
Costs vary by country — the US and UK are expensive (₹40–80L total), while Germany, France, and some Canadian provinces have near-zero tuition. But here's the real question: post-degree salaries abroad (especially in tech, finance, and healthcare) can range from ₹50–1.5Cr per year. With smart scholarship hunting, education loans, and the right country choice, the ROI is typically achieved within 2–3 years of working. We'll help you run the actual numbers for your situation.
Do I need GRE/GMAT? What if my score is low?
Many universities — including top-50 programs — have gone GRE/GMAT optional post-2020, and many more never required it. For countries like Germany, Ireland, and parts of Canada, GRE is rarely needed at all. If you do have a low score, we help you identify universities that weigh other factors more heavily, and we'll guide you on whether a retake makes strategic sense for your timeline.
How long does the entire process take?
Typically 6–12 months from your first counselling session to your visa approval. Here's a rough breakdown: 1–2 months for profile building + test prep, 2–3 months for applications and SOPs, 1–2 months for decisions and scholarship negotiations, and 2–3 months for visa processing. We build a personalised timeline with you in your very first session so you always know what's next.
What if I get rejected? Do you offer refunds?
We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee on all plans. More importantly, we strategically diversify your application list across safe, target, and reach universities — so rejection from one or two schools is built into the plan. Our median student gets accepted into 4–6 universities. In the extremely rare case of zero admits, our team will work with you at no extra cost to explore alternate intake options.
Can I get an education loan without collateral?
Yes — and this is something we specialise in. We have partnerships with 15+ lenders who offer non-collateral loans up to ₹50 lakhs for admitted students at accredited universities. Collateral-based loans can go much higher. Our loan advisors help you compare options, prepare the application, and even negotiate interest rates — we've gotten students rates as low as 9.5% per annum.
